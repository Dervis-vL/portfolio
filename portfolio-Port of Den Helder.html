Design of new quay wall

Back

Due to the rapidly increading corrosion processes of metals by MIC (Microbiologically Influenced Corrosion), the quay wall at hte Port of Den Helder needs to be renewed. The deterioration of the pile sheets no longer guarantees a safe structure.

The objective - Calculate and design a new structure that functions as a quay wall and is able to protect metal against the MIC.

The tech used in the calculations and design of the structure are Scia Engineer, Idea StatiCa, Mathcad and Excel.

Technologies: SCIA ENGINEER, IDEA STATICA, Mathcad, EXCEL
