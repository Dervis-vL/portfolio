Restructure of the A9

As part of the restructuring of the highway A9 in the Netherlands, between Badhoevedorp and Holendrecht, I have been part of the calculations of a new viaduct but also recalculations and strengthening measures for several existing viaducts.

The design level of the calculations are in the Definitive Design and according to the European norm and standards.

Technologies: SCIA ENGINEER, IDEA STATICA, EXCEL
