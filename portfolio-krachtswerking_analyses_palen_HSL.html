Analysis pile foundation of HSL Rijpwetering

Back

Deformations habe been measured to the structure Rijpwetering in the HSL. Therefore an analysis is performed to determine the safety of the current and future situation according to the norm. Several non-linear models with different load configurations, varying in spring constant, were made to determine the transmission of forces.

The objective - Use Python to parse the XML data from several non-linear Scia Engineer models, to then combine forces to determine the enveloping tension in the concrete pile foundation.

The piles could then be tested by performing a unity check in the section.

Technologies: SCIA ENGINEER, IDEA STATICA, Python, EXCEL, XML
