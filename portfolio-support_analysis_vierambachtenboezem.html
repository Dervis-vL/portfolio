Bridge bearing analysis

Back

A bridge with a lifespan of 100 years and build in 2010 is showing signs of failing bearings. More than half of the rubber bearings have shown significant displacement.

My analysis concerned finding a solution to fix the problem. This is done by inspecting the current state of the structure and the archive data to find the cause.

By determining the cause I was then able do a feasability studie for solutions.
